Frequently Asked Questions

theDalewordweft is a series of online learning experiences built to nurture creative thinking, confidence, and collaboration among young minds. Each workshop focuses on a different skill area—from diplomacy and debate to communication, design, and leadership—giving participants an interactive and practical way to learn beyond textbooks.

The series launches with Munterra—our hands-on Model UN starter that builds diplomacy, debate, and leadership. Upcoming workshops in theDalewordweft will explore creative communication, critical analysis, and storytelling—each crafted to help participants express, lead, and collaborate effectively.

theDalewordweft workshops are open to students, learners, and young professionals who want to explore new skills and perspectives in a structured, engaging, and mentor-guided format. No prior experience is needed; every session is beginner-friendly yet deeply enriching.
