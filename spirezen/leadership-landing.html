Leadership
Acceleration Program

A School-Based Orientation & Skill-Building Initiative

Overview

The Leadership Acceleration Program (LAP) is an orientation-based initiative conducted in schools and colleges to nurture leadership, diplomacy, communication, and holistic self-development through interactive sessions.

Objectives

  • Leadership & diplomacy fundamentals
  • Confidence & decision-making
  • Public speaking & communication
  • Responsible & global thinking

Target Audience

  • Grade 6 onwards
  • Higher secondary students
  • College students
  • Student leaders & councils

Program Format

  • 60–120 min sessions
  • In-school orientation
  • Classroom / Auditorium
  • Interactive & activity-based

Core Focus Areas

Leadership foundations, initiative, responsibility, ethical leadership, and understanding leadership beyond titles.
